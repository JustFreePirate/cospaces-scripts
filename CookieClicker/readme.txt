Cookie clicker guide 
When you click at cookie you get +1 coin.
You can buy boyscouts, grannyies and bake houses just clicking them and they will get coins for you with the same speed.
You can buy upgrades, that locates behind your back, and boyscouts, grannies or houses will get x2 coins per second.
The goal is collect 1 000 000 coins as fast as you can. Record is 155 seconds. 
Big granny, big boyscout and big house shows how many coins you have to spent to buy another one granny, BS or house.
Cookie shows two things: your income per second from all peoples and houses that you have and how many coins you have at this time.
